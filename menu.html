New Menu Every Week!

Apricity changes it's menu every week to feature the items our hunter and gatherer team brought back from the Appalachian mountains. All ingredients not hunted or gathered were grown or raised by our homestead team living on the family owned property. Here is this week's menu selection!

Breakfast

Scrambled Eggs with Wild Salsa Verde

  • Locally sourced chicken eggs made -to-order and drizzled with salasa verde made from ground ivy, sorrel, and leeks

*Acorn Pancakes

  • Pancakes made from nad ground acorn flour. Can be topped with apples, pecans, homemade maple syrup, and honey.

*Corn Meal Porridge

  • A sweet Jamacian porridge made form hand-ground cornmeal flavored with brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla, and nutmeg.
Lunch

Rabbit Chickweed Ceasar Salad

  • Crispy or Grilled rabbit on a bed of lettuce and chickweed. Served with homemade ceasar salad dressing.

Stuffed Dock

  • Rolled dock leaves stuffed with wild cought trout salad.

Chipotle Bison Burger

  • Made-To-Order grilled bison served with homemade chipotle sauce, chickweed, tomato, and onion. Served on bread of choice.
Dinner

Wild Herb Salmon

  • Wild caught salmon grilled with a ground ivey wild herb rub. Served with anciet graines and a red dead-nettle salad.

Butter-Basted Elk Steak

  • Marinated elk tenderloin cooked with a skillet over a traditional wood fire. Served with sweet potato mash.

Wild Gumbo

  • Vegetable broth slow stewed with peppers, wild onions, celery, tomatoes, mushrooms, leeks, carrots, shrimp, duck, deer, chicken, and wild rice.
*Menu Items may contain recipes using milk from homestead cows and goats. These items can be replaced with a dairy-free alternative for an additional fee.
